Welcome to Games Revisited

A (sometimes agonising) journey back through the video games we grew up on and love(d)

We are not doing this for the inevitable, illustrious riches and fame - just the love of games

We set ourselves a challenge - to go back and play games that we thought were the 'best games ever' at the time and see if they prove as enjoyable as they once were.

Feel free to disagree - if you have a dissenting opinion to one of our reviews, get in touch. If you're not an abusive troll, we might let you do our job - lucky, lucky you!

Updated Mondays at 9am (British Time)
