Silent Hill 2 2001 97% We will fight you to death and/or smother you with a pillow in your sleep if you disagree with us. A dark twist towards the end that makes sense, and some of the best writing and voice acting in videogame history. Silent Hill 2 is a game EVERYBODY should play at least once. Without getting too deep into spoiler territory, we're big fans of that ending. You know the one. Woof.
Star Wars : Knights of The Old Republic 2003 95% An original wildcard entry that placed it 3rd, and then a slightly over enthusiastic review put KOTOR at number one last year. Depth and breadth with a plot twist we didn’t see coming in 2003 makes Star Wars the most enjoyable RPG. It helps that it is Star Wars, it helps there is fan service, and it helps that it is in it’s own time period and doesn’t try to fit in prequel nonsense. Basically anything that HK-47 says, is or does.
